How to Join Google Classroom

Follow these steps to join your classes on Google Classroom:

  1. Go to Google Classroom.
  2. Make sure you are logged in with your official school email address.
  3. Click the "+" icon (Join or create class) in the top right corner.
  4. Select "Join class".
  5. Enter the class code for your subject:
    • Network Communication: k7hyphaz
    • Operating Systems: macdas6f
    • Programming Languages: nvotrxwm
    • Software Engineering 1: f2s2jmwp
    • Parallel and Distributed Computing: d5stuwqn
  6. Click "Join" to enroll in the class.

You can also click on the "Join Class (Google Classroom)" links directly on the schedule page.
